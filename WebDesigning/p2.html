Welcome To Chandubhai S Patel Institute of Technology

Chandubhai S Patel Institute of Technology, is the first institute established in year 2000 at Education Campus, Changa (now CHARUSAT). The institute is managed through a think tank of academician, scientists, engineers and professionals from all parts of the world. Started with 240 seats with four Bachelor Degree Engineering Programs in the year 2000, the institute now has total intake of 690 seats in 7 programs of Bachelor Degree and 108 seats of Post Graduate Degree.
